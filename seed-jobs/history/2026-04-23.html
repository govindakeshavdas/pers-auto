The technical challenge (structured logging + compliance-grade report generation + evidence linking) is tractable. The distribution challenge is harder — you need to integrate with every agent framework. But Adaptional, Agentin, and every insurance/banking/healthcare AI company is a potential customer. No incumbent is focused on this. Worthwhile — clear demand signal from multiple verticals, tractable engineering, and no funded competitor.
  • AI-maximalist SaaS holding company (Rocketable space): Unique opportunity, binary outcome. This is either a category-creating bet or a complete miss. The thesis depends entirely on AI capabilities continuing to improve. You can't compete with Rocketable by building the same thing — the advantage comes from the portfolio of acquired products. The opportunity for an engineer is joining Rocketable itself, not building a competitor. Not worthwhile as a competitive entry — but joining Rocketable as a founding engineer is one of the most interesting engineering bets available.
