Crowding: BlackLine ($3B+, month-end close), FloQast ($100M+ raised), Numeric ($23M raised), Moby Analytics (Run 26, audit agents). Basis differentiates on the AI-native approach to the entire close process vs. incremental automation of individual steps.

Build a tool for DTC e-commerce brands: (1) enter competitor URLs, (2) AI builds scrapers that monitor competitor prices, inventory status, review ratings, and new product launches daily, (3) delivers a structured dashboard showing price changes, stockouts, and competitive moves. The e-commerce buyer (product manager or merchandiser) makes fast purchasing decisions and pays $100-300/month. Distribute via Shopify App Store and e-commerce Twitter/LinkedIn. $100-300/month × 30 brands = $3K-9K MRR. The ROI: "know the moment your competitor drops their price on your top-selling product."

The regulatory tailwind is real: EU AI Act enforces August 2026, ISO AI exclusions are becoming standard, and AI-related lawsuits are up 137%. Every company deploying AI agents WILL need to test them, but most don't need or want insurance yet — they need the risk report first. Build a self-serve tool: (1) connect your agent's API endpoint, (2) AI runs 50+ probes (prompt injection, data exfiltration, decision drift, PII leakage, jailbreak), (3) generates a risk scorecard with specific failure modes and remediation, (4) provides a "tested" badge for your docs/procurement page. $99-299/month per agent endpoint × 30 agents = $3K-9K MRR.
